What is a Holistic Life Health Coach?

A holistic life coach integrates emotions, mind, body and spirit. They help you identify and reach your goals by breaking down the barriers that are preventing you from finding your full potential. Holistic Life coaching can involve working on various aspects of your life, such as your relationships, career, time management, balance and spiritual growth. A life coach starts from where you are today and helps you map out a plan for getting to where you want to be.

As your Life Coach, I want to celebrate your individuality,whether you want help moving forward in an area of your life or you want to turn your passion into purpose. I serve as your motivator, strategist, and accountability partner. I will work with you one-on-one to assist you in determining and achieving your personal or professional goals. I will help you to clarify your dreams and recognize what is standing in your way. Together, we will discover and release your inner warrior!

It is important to state that a Health/Wellness coach is NOT a nutritionist or an MD. I cannot medicate, test, prescribe supplements or prescribe specific dietary meal plans. For those types of requirements, I recommend you seek out a qualified nutritionist, naturopath or an MD.

It is important to state what a life coach is NOT a therapist; I do not directly treat issues like depression or mental illness. I recommend you seek out a qualified therapist to complement your coaching, and I can provide recommendations.

What is a Health/Wellness Coach?

A Health/Wellness Coach focuses on all aspects of your health and wellness and is someone who empowers you to make lasting transformative changes. Health/Wellness Coaches allow you to develop healthy habits, motivation to act, and to create meaningful and lasting changes for a better today and for a healthy future!

As your Health/Wellness Coach, I want to help you look at all aspects of your life to help you identify any areas that may be holding you back from reaching your health goals. Whether your goal is to increase energy, lose weight, or improve overall wellness, I can help you achieve this. By working with you directly, we'll discover what is right for you and what works best for you together. Working with a Health/Wellness Coach is one of the best gifts that you can give yourself. You'll learn to build healthy habits that line up with how you want to feel every day! I'm here to help you with planning, action, accountability, and follow-through.
